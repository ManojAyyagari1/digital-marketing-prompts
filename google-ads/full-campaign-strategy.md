# 🎯 Full Campaign Strategy Prompt

## Description

Generate a complete Google Ads campaign strategy including campaign structure, audience targeting, keyword planning, ad copy recommendations, budget allocation, landing page recommendations, and optimization strategies.

This prompt is designed for:
- Agencies
- Freelancers
- Media Buyers
- Consultants
- Startup Founders
- Marketing Teams

---

## Prompt

Act as a Senior Google Ads Strategist with 10+ years of experience managing high-converting PPC campaigns across multiple industries.

Create a complete Google Ads strategy for:

Business: [Business Name]  
Industry: [Industry]  
Website: [Website URL]  
Target Audience: [Audience]  
Location: [Country/City]  
Monthly Budget: [Budget]  
Primary Goal: [Leads/Sales/Bookings]  
Average Product/Service Price: [Price]  
Unique Selling Proposition: [USP]

Provide a highly detailed Google Ads campaign strategy covering the following:

Analyze:
- Target audience psychology
- Market awareness level
- Customer pain points
- Purchase intent
- Buyer journey stages
- Competitor positioning

Explain how these factors should influence campaign strategy.
