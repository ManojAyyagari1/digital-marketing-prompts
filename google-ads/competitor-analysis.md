# 🕵️ Competitor Analysis Prompt

## Description

Analyze competitor Google Ads strategies and identify market opportunities.

---

## Prompt

Act as a PPC competitor research expert.

Analyze the Google Ads strategy likely used by competitors in the [industry] niche.

Provide insights on:
- Keyword targeting patterns
- Ad messaging
- Funnel structure
- Offer positioning
- Retargeting strategies
- Landing page structures
- Psychological triggers
- Pricing angles
- Promotional strategies

Then identify:
- Market gaps
- Weaknesses competitors may have
- Positioning opportunities
- Messaging improvements
- Untapped keyword opportunities

Also suggest:
- Ways to outperform competitors
- Unique differentiators
- Creative angles
- Better offers
