# 🔍 Keyword Research Prompt

## Description

Generate a complete Google Ads keyword research and intent mapping strategy.

---

## Prompt

Act as a senior PPC keyword strategist.

Generate a complete Google Ads keyword research plan for:

Business: [Business Name]  
Industry: [Industry]  
Location: [Location]  
Target Audience: [Audience]

Organize keywords into:

## High Intent Keywords
Keywords with strong buying intent.

## Commercial Investigation Keywords
Users comparing products/services.

## Local Intent Keywords
Location-based searches.

## Long-Tail Keywords
Low competition, high conversion opportunities.

## Competitor Keywords
Searches related to competitors.

## Question-Based Keywords
Searches users ask before converting.

For each keyword provide:
- Search intent
- Funnel stage
- Competition level
- Match type recommendation
- Suggested CPC level

Also include:
- Negative keywords
- Suggested ad groups
- Keyword expansion opportunities
- Search term optimization recommendations

Explain:
- Which keywords should receive highest budget priority
- Which keywords are likely to convert best
- Which keywords are better for awareness vs conversion
