# 🎯 Full Meta Ads Strategy Prompt

## What This Does

Creates a complete Meta Ads strategy including:
- Funnel structure
- Audience targeting
- Creative direction
- Budget allocation
- Retargeting
- Scaling framework

Perfect for:
- Agencies
- Freelancers
- eCommerce brands
- Lead generation businesses
- Coaches & consultants

---

## Prompt

"Act as a Senior Meta Ads Strategist with experience scaling profitable Facebook and Instagram campaigns.

Create a complete Meta Ads strategy for:

Business: [Business Name]  
Industry: [Industry]  
Offer: [Offer]  
Website: [Website URL]  
Target Audience: [Audience]  
Location: [Location]  
Monthly Budget: ₹[Budget]  
Goal: [Leads/Sales/Appointments]

Provide:

# 1. Funnel Strategy
Create:
- TOFU campaigns
- MOFU campaigns
- BOFU campaigns
- Retargeting campaigns

Explain:
- objective of each stage
- budget allocation
- expected audience behavior

---

# 2. Audience Strategy
Include:
- interests
- behaviors
- lookalikes
- broad targeting
- exclusions

Explain:
- which audiences are likely to convert first
- scaling opportunities
- audience testing order

---

# 3. Creative Strategy
Generate:
- image ad ideas
- video ad ideas
- UGC concepts
- carousel concepts
- hooks

Explain:
- emotional triggers
- scroll-stopping mechanisms
- winning creative psychology

---

# 4. Ad Copy Strategy
Generate frameworks for:
- hooks
- primary text
- headlines
- CTA styles

Use:
- curiosity
- urgency
- authority
- social proof
- transformation angles

---

# 5. Retargeting Strategy
Create campaigns for:
- website visitors
- video viewers
- add-to-cart users
- Instagram engagers

Include:
- retargeting windows
- messaging variations
- offer sequencing

---

# 6. Optimization Framework
Create:
- daily optimization checklist
- weekly optimization checklist
- monthly optimization checklist

Cover:
- CTR optimization
- CPA reduction
- ROAS improvement
- creative fatigue management

---

# 7. Scaling Strategy
Explain:
- vertical scaling
- horizontal scaling
- creative scaling
- audience expansion

Include:
- risk management
- scaling thresholds
- performance monitoring

Present the response like a professional Meta Ads strategy document."

---

## Use Case

Use before launching any major Meta Ads campaign.

Most campaigns fail because there’s no structured strategy.

This prompt helps:
- improve campaign clarity
- increase ROAS
- reduce wasted spend
- scale faster
