# 🕵️ Competitor Analysis Prompt

## What This Does

Breaks down competitor Meta ads to uncover:
- targeting strategy
- hook frameworks
- positioning
- emotional triggers
- offer strategy

---

## Prompt

"I found this Meta ad from my competitor.

Ad Details:
[Describe the ad: visual, copy, CTA, offer]

My Product:
[Your Product]

My USP:
[Your USP]

Tear down the ad completely.

Analyze:

# 1. Strategy
- What marketing strategy are they using?
- Which funnel stage is this ad targeting?
- What awareness level are they speaking to?

---

# 2. Audience
- Who are they likely targeting?
- What pain points are they addressing?
- What emotions are they triggering?

---

# 3. Creative Analysis
Explain:
- why the visual works
- what makes the hook effective
- what scrolling pattern interruption they use
- what psychological triggers are activated

---

# 4. Weaknesses
Identify:
- weak messaging
- missing objections
- poor CTA
- unclear positioning
- creative weaknesses

---

# 5. Improvement Strategy
Show me how to create a BETTER ad.

Generate:
- 3 stronger hook ideas
- 3 better positioning angles
- better CTA ideas
- stronger emotional angles

Explain:
- why these improvements would outperform the competitor."

---

## Use Case

Use while browsing Meta Ad Library.

---

## Why This Matters

Most marketers copy competitors blindly.

This prompt helps:
- understand WHY ads work
- create differentiated campaigns
- discover market gaps
